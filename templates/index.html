About Me

Professional

I have worked in the I.T. industry for almost 10 years. My interests range from systems administration, systems architecture, software development, and automating infrastructure and software deployments. I have spent the last several years moving from a support and administrator role into a DevOps and site reliability engineer role.

Today, I still work on the infrastructure side, but I write and build software and administer the "cloud native" side of things. I work with Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, GCP, AWS, and Go primarily in my day to day.

At night, I take on a different role. The call of operating system and kernel development has become deafening. So I am pursuing that in my free time. The primary focus is C userland utilities and then evolve into a kernel and simple operating system. Eventually, I would like to make that my full time job.
